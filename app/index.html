Key Features

1. Power

Using FFT, analyze and compare the spectrum of any EEG two channels and apply Wavelet Subband breakdown to inspect the individual known frequency subband signals (Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta and Gamma) making up the channel data.

2. Coherence

Rapidly scrutinize inter- and/or intra-hemispheric communications using Coherence maps. The tool comprises a sensitivity bar with a threshold marker as well as application of coherence calculations on known frequency subbands and the composite channel data.

3. ICA

Visualize the regions of highest electrophysiological activity through the Independent Component Analysis. Using blind source separation, detected sources are back projected onto the electrode map to examine influence of each sources on all electrodes. Any portion of the EEG recording can be investigated for the most powerful sources conjointly with the overall effect of all sources.

4. DSA

The density spectral array acts as a fast way to picture changes in the frequency composition of EEG channel data. The investigator views the dominant frequency ranges through a color spectrum scaled to the lower and higher powers of frequency ranges in individual and in all channels.
